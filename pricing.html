Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really no credit card required for the free trial?
Correct — you can start a 14-day Pro trial with just your email address. No payment details are collected until you choose to upgrade at the end of your trial.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. Monthly Pro subscriptions can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. Annual plans can be cancelled at renewal.
How accurate is the AI CAPEX Predictor?
The model achieves a mean absolute percentage error (MAPE) of ±3.2% on our holdout validation set of 240+ projects. It is trained on 2,400+ real-world mining projects from 1990–2026 and retrained monthly with new data. Results are indicative and should be validated by a qualified engineer for investment decisions.
Do you offer discounts for universities or non-profits?
Yes — we offer 50% discounts for verified university departments and accredited research institutions, and 30% for registered non-profit organisations. Contact us at hello@sesmine.com with your institution details.
What data sources power the commodity prices?
Real-time prices are sourced from LME, COMEX, CME and SGX exchange feeds, supplemented by spot market data from major commodity brokers. Prices are updated every 15 minutes during market hours.
Is my company's data kept private and secure?
Absolutely. All user data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Your project data, forecasts and company information are never shared with other users or used to train our models. We are GDPR and Australian Privacy Act compliant. Enterprise customers can request a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
